New York

Location

New York is located in the northeastern United States, on the east coast. It is bordered by the states of New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Connecticut, Massachusetts, and Vermont.

About

New York City, the most populous city in the state, is often referred to as "The Big Apple." It is a global hub for finance, culture, entertainment, and fashion.

Weather

New York experiences four distinct seasons. Summers are warm with average temperatures ranging from 70°F to 85°F (21°C to 29°C). Winters can be cold, with temperatures averaging between 25°F to 40°F (-4°C to 4°C) and occasional snowfall.

Population

New York is one of the most populous states in the United States, with an estimated population of over 19 million residents. The majority of the population resides in the New York City metropolitan area.
